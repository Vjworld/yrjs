Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

TL;DR: YRJS does not collect your data on any server. Everything stays in your browser. Google Sheets sync goes directly to your own Google account.

1. What Data We Collect

YRJS stores the following data exclusively on your device (browser localStorage/IndexedDB):

  • Your account details (name, email) — hashed locally
  • Trip information and expense records
  • App settings and preferences
  • Loyalty coin balance

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not operate any backend server or cloud database
  • We do not send your expense data to any third-party service (except Google Sheets, which you own)
  • We do not use cookies for tracking
  • We do not sell your data

3. Google Sheets Integration

When you use the Google Sheets sync feature, your data is sent directly from your browser to your own Google Apps Script deployment, which writes to a Google Sheet in your personal Google Drive. YRJS never intercepts or stores this data.

4. Analytics

YRJS may use privacy-respecting, cookie-free analytics (such as Plausible or Fathom) in future versions to understand how users interact with the app. This will be disclosed in an update to this policy.

5. Third-Party Links

YRJS may contain links to partner sponsor websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

6. Children's Privacy

YRJS is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

Privacy concerns? Email: privacy@yrjs.app
